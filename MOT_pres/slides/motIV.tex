Динамика количества атомов в МОЛ:
\begin{equation*}
	\frac{d N}{d t} = \sub{\Phi}{load} - \cancel{\gamma N} - \beta N^2
	\hspace{0.5cm} \Rightarrow \hspace{0.5cm}
	N(t) = \sqrt{\frac{\sub{\Phi}{load}}{\beta}} \left(1-e^{-t \sqrt{\beta \sub{\Phi}{load}}}\right)
\end{equation*}
